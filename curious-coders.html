Curious Coders: Interactive Learning for Kids

Duration: 6 months

Frequency: 2 classes per week (Weekends and Weekdays)

Total Hours: 48 hours of live classes per course

Our Curious Coders program is designed to engage children with fun and educational activities in both robotics and coding. The course covers two main components:

Course Components

Junior Robotics Course:

  • Dive into the basics of robotics
  • Learn to build and program simple robots using an intuitive kit.
  • Build projects like Football robot, Guest counter, Gear Crane and many more.
  • Fee: Rs. 7500 per month (includes robotics kit).

Coding with Scratch Jr.:

  • Explore the fundamentals of coding using the Scratch Jr. platform.
  • Develop problem-solving skills and creativity through fun coding projects.
  • Build various animations, Games and Stories.
  • Fee: Rs. 5500 per month.

Why Choose Curious Coders?

  • No Prerequisites: This course requires no prior knowledge, making it perfect for young beginners.
  • Interactive Learning: Our classes are designed to be engaging and interactive, ensuring that children have fun while learning.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: With a mix of robotics and coding, children gain a well-rounded introduction to technology.
  • Live Classes: Real-time instruction from experienced educators to guide your child's learning journey.
  • Hands-On Projects: Over 100 activities that encourage practical application of concepts learned in class.

Skills Developed

  • Basic Programming: Introduction to coding concepts with Scratch Jr.
  • Robotics: Understanding basic robotics and engineering principles.
  • Problem-Solving: Developing critical thinking through coding challenges and robotics tasks.
  • Creativity: Encouraging innovation and creative thinking.
  • Collaboration: Learning teamwork through group activities and projects.
  • Confidence: Building self-confidence by accomplishing tech projects.
