Creative Builders Program

Duration: 6 months

Frequency: 2 classes per week (Weekends and Weekdays)

Total Hours: 48 hours of live classes per course

Creative Builders, an innovative program designed for children aged 8 to 11. This program aims to cultivate a deep interest in technology through engaging and interactive courses in Robotics, Coding, Electronics, Python, and AI for beginners. With live classes twice a week, your child will enjoy nearly 48 hours of immersive learning and hands-on activities.

Course Components

Robotics:

  • Dive into the world of robotics and learn to build and program robots using specialised kits.
  • Basic understanding of mechanical concepts.
  • Problem-solving, engineering design, teamwork.
  • Line Follower Robot, Obstacle Avoidance Robot and many more.
  • Fee: Rs. 7500 per month.

Coding with Scratch:

  • Explore the fundamentals of coding with Scratch, a visual programming language designed for young learners.
  • Basic computer skills.
  • Logical thinking, creativity, sequencing.
  • Interactive Stories, Simple Games.
  • Fee: Rs. 5500 per month.

Circuit Explorers:

  • Learn the basics of electronics and circuitry with hands-on projects.
  • Basic understanding of circuits.
  • Circuit design, troubleshooting, creativity.
  • Light Sensor, Alarm System.
  • Fee: Rs. 7000 per month.

Python:

  • An introduction to Python programming, suitable for beginners.
  • Basic coding knowledge.
  • Computational thinking, programming logic, problem-solving.
  • Simple Calculator, Basic Game.
  • Cost: Rs. 5500 per month.

AI for Beginners:

  • Discover the basics of Artificial Intelligence and its applications.
  • Basic coding and mathematical skills.
  • Analytical thinking, understanding AI concepts, creativity.
  • Chatbot, Image Recognition, etc.
  • Fee: Rs. 7500 per month.

Program Highlights

  • Interactive Learning: Engage in 70+ hands-on activities that make learning fun and effective.
  • Expert Instructors: Learn from experienced professionals who are passionate about teaching.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: Cover a wide range of topics and skills essential for future tech leaders.
  • Supportive Environment: Foster a love for learning in a nurturing and encouraging atmosphere.
