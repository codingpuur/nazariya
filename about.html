Nazariya Story

Nazariya was born from a deep passion for education and a desire to make a difference. During her journey, Neha discovered that over 90% of people in her community were unaware of the transformative power of tech education, a field she had dedicated a decade of her career to.

Nazariya is more than just a learning space—it's a movement to bring cutting-edge education to every corner of the country. We celebrate the importance of teaching, and we're committed to delivering knowledge to everyone who seeks it and deserves it. At Nazariya, we believe in nurturing curiosity, encouraging exploration, and helping young minds think big.

Our Mission

Our goal is to shape the future by opening new paths of learning and creativity, transforming dreams into tangible achievements and ensuring that every young mind has equal opportunities to become a trailblazer in the digital era.

Nazariya is a learning studio dedicated to nurturing young minds through innovative and experiential education. Our mission is to empower children with essential 21st-century skills, fostering creativity, critical thinking, and a passion for lifelong learning.
